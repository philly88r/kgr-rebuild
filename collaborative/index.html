A Win-Win Partnership

Coaching Collaborative

A unique program that creates meaningful opportunities for both clients and KGR graduates. Accessible education and health coaching for everyone.

What led to the creation of the Collaborative?

It has always been KGR’s mission to be a place where both education and health coaching opportunities are affordable and accessible. The Coaching Collaborative expands access for clients while supporting KGR graduates in completing their required 50 coaching sessions to sit for the National Board (NBHWC) exam.

For Clients

Receive free access to high-quality coaching support to help you feel your best.

For Coaches

Gain valuable practical experience and fulfill national board requirements.

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the KGR Coaching Collaborative.

Is there a cost to enroll as a client?

There is no cost to enroll! KGR Coaching Collaborative is designed to offer free access to coaching support for clients interested in experiencing health coaching first hand.

How qualified is my coach?

Your coach is a KGR-certified coach who has successfully completed a National Board for Health & Wellness Coaching-approved training program. They have met the required educational and practical training standards.

When can I enroll as a coach?

You are eligible for enrollment as soon as you receive your KGR certifying letter stating you have completed all the necessary requirements to sit for the NBHWC national exam.

“By becoming a member you are helping graduates attain required coaching sessions. It’s a win-win!”
