A Win-Win Partnership

Coaching Collaborative

A unique program that pairs clients with KGR graduates. Clients get wellness coaching support, while graduates complete their required 50 coaching sessions to sit for the National Board exam.

The Why

What led to the creation of the Collaborative?

It has always been KGR's mission to be a place where both education and health coaching opportunities are affordable and accessible. The Coaching Collaborative expands access for clients while supporting KGR graduates in completing their required 50 coaching sessions to sit for the National Board (NBHWC) exam.

KGR hopes to grow the program and partner with non-profit organizations to expand further to those who might not otherwise be able to afford health coaching services.

For Clients

Receive free access to high-quality coaching support to help you feel your best. A KGR team member carefully matches each client with a graduate coach.

For Coaches

Gain valuable practical experience and fulfill the NBHWC requirement of 50 coaching sessions to sit for the national exam.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

For Coaches

How do I enroll as a coach?
After completing and graduating from the KGR certification course, fill out the Coaches form on the page. A KGR team member will then contact you with next steps to connect you with a client.
When can I enroll as a coach?
You are eligible for enrollment as soon as you receive your KGR certifying letter stating you have completed all the necessary requirements to sit for the NBHWC national exam.
Can I refer clients to the program?
Yes — referrals from family and friends are welcomed. Since coaches cannot directly coach their own family and friends, referrals give loved ones access to quality coaching while other coaches gain experience.
Can I also be a client in the program?
Yes. Coaches can fill out the Clients form and KGR will strategically place you with a coach outside of your cohort to meet NBHWC exam session requirements.

For Clients

How do I enroll as a client?
Fill out the Clients form on the page, and a KGR team member will reach out to connect you with a coach.
Is there a cost to enroll as a client?
No cost. The program is designed to offer free access to coaching support for clients interested in experiencing health coaching first hand.
How qualified is my coach?
Your coach is a KGR-certified coach who has successfully completed a National Board for Health & Wellness Coaching-approved training program. They have met the required educational and practical training standards and are fully KGR-certified.
Can clients refer others to the program?
Yes — all referrals are welcome, as serving more clients strengthens the program's impact.

"By becoming a member you are helping graduates attain required coaching sessions. It's a win-win!"
