You Belong.

Diversity, Equity, Inclusion, Accessibility, and Justice

Diversity and inclusion reach far beyond buzzwords at KGR. We understand that the cornerstone of a thriving, equitable society lies within the great responsibility of diversity, equity, inclusion, accessibility, and Justice (DEIAJ).

We strive everyday to recognize and value the unique backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences our students bring to the table. Our mission is to create an environment where everyone feels welcome, safe, brave, and empowered to be uniquely themselves.

Exposure to diverse perspectives in the classroom challenges our preconceptions and encourages critical thinking. At KGR, we are intentional about bringing these rich conversations into the classroom and coaching framework.

“

A single action, no matter how bold, won’t change the collective nature of racism, homophobia, transphobia, sexism, or ablism. But everyday actions are not insignificant. They matter because life isn’t lived at the collective level. It’s lived at the individual level, where every day actions make the difference between inclusion or exclusion, joy or pain, opportunity or disadvantage.

Suzy Levy, Mind the Inclusion Gap
