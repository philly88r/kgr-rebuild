Who We Are

At KGR, we are passionate about the health coaching industry and the positive impact it can have on people's lives. We believe that health coaching has the potential to transform the way we approach health and wellness, by providing a personalized, holistic approach that addresses the root causes of health issues.

Our Mission

Empowering students through transformative educational experiences

KGR aspires to lead in empowering students, serving as a bridge to opportunities, and delivering transformative educational experiences that change lives and fortify the bonds of our shared humanity. Our mission is to unlock access to premium, inclusive health coaching programs that break down barriers, amplify student achievement, cultivate a workforce skilled in global and multicultural practices, and enhance the well-being and quality of life for individuals everywhere.

Leadership

Our Founders

Beth Knott, Au.D., NBC-HWC

Founder, Director of Curriculum and Development

Dr. Beth Knott is a National Board Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC) and owner of Beth Knott Health Coaching, LLC. She coaches private and corporate clients and instructs at Randolph Community College's Health and Wellness Coaching Certification Program. She gives presentations on Active Listening, Mindfulness and Stress Management. Beth holds a doctorate in Audiology and was a pediatric audiologist for 20 years before transitioning to health coaching. She serves on community boards including the Asheboro City Board of Education. Her motto: "Everyone deserves to feel amazing. One small step at a time!"

Daffie Garris, NBC-HWC

Founder, Chief Financial Officer

Daffie Garris is a co-founder of KGR and Owner of Garris Coaching. An NBC-HWC and Certified Human Resource Professional (SHPR and SHRM-SCP), she provided executive leadership in higher education for over 27 years within the NC Community College System, her final role being CFO/VP for Administrative Services. Her career began as faculty and Division Chair. She helps entrepreneurs build coaching businesses and believes education is the key to changing lives. Outside work she enjoys hiking, kayaking, and outdoor activities.

Rae Ritter, NBC-HWC

Founder, Director of Information and Technology

Rae is an NBC-HWC and owner of Live Moore Coaching. Previously worked in nutrition and epidemiology research at UNC. A personal healing journey led her through studies of eating habits and related diseases, inspiring her career in health coaching. Rae has helped clients reverse disease processes, lose weight, and eliminate toxic substances and relationships. She offers unconditional positive support and non-judgmental encouragement to help clients establish lasting, self-directed changes.
