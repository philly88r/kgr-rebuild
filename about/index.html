Who we are

At KGR, we are passionate about the health coaching industry and the positive impact it can have on people's lives. We believe that health coaching has the potential to transform the way we approach health and wellness, by providing a personalized, holistic approach that addresses the root causes of health issues.

Our Mission

Empowering students through transformative educational experiences

KGR aspires to lead in empowering students, serving as a bridge to opportunities, and delivering transformative educational experiences that change lives and fortify the bonds of our shared humanity. Our mission at KGR is to unlock access to premium, inclusive health coaching programs that break down barriers, amplify student achievement, cultivate a workforce skilled in global and multicultural practices, and enhance the well-being and quality of life for individuals everywhere.

Our Founders

Beth Knott, Au.D., NBC-HWC

Founder, Director of Curriculum and Development

Dr. Beth Knott is a National Board Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC). and owner of Beth Knott Health Coaching, LLC. In addition to coaching private clients, Beth coaches at the corporate level and has also provided coaching services for currently enrolled students at Randolph Community College in Asheboro, NC. She serves as an instructor for Randolph Community College's Health and Wellness Coaching Certification Program. In addition to coaching, Beth can be found giving presentations and leading group courses on Active Listening, Mindfulness and Stress Management. Beth holds a doctorate degree in Audiology and served as a pediatric audiologist for 20 years before shifting into health coaching full time. She is highly engaged in her community and serves on a variety of community boards. She is currently fulfilling her second term on the Asheboro City Board of Education. Her motto is: Everyone deserves to feel amazing. One small step at a time!

Daffie Garris, NBC-HWC

Founder, Chief Financial Officer

Daffie Garris is a proud partner in KGR Health and Wellness Academy, as well as the Owner of Garris Coaching. Daffie is a National Board Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC). She is also a Certified Human Resource Professional-SHPR and SHRM-SCP. She believes in constantly learning and growing and helps others do the same. She provided executive leadership in higher education for over twenty-seven years for the North Carolina Community College System. Her last role was Chief Financial Officer/Vice President for Administrative Services. However, her higher education career started as a faculty member and Division Chair. Her first love has always been connecting with students through teaching. She loves to combine her business knowledge with teaching by helping those entrepreneurs who are dreaming of owning their own successful coaching business. She believes education is the key to changing lives. When not teaching and coaching, you will find her hiking, kayaking and many other outdoor activities.

Rae Ritter, NBC-HWC

Founder, Director of Information and Technology

Rae is a National Board Certified Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC) and owner of Live Moore Coaching. In addition to coaching private clients, Rae teaches others to become coaches and spent many years working in nutrition and epidemiology research at the University of North Carolina. Rae's personal healing experience led to a journey through studies of eating habits and related diseases, and ultimately inspired Rae to become a health and wellness coach and teacher. Rae has helped clients reverse disease processes, lose weight, and eliminate toxic substances and relationships by providing each with unconditional positive support as they establish self-directed, lasting changes designed to suit their specific needs. Rae's non-judgmental encouragement creates a respectful space for others to transform into the person they aspire to be.

Stephanie Cuthbertson

Lead Instructor & Corporate Coach

Stephanie had always been captivated by the intricate dance of life within the human body. With a degree in Biology from Columbia University, she spent years delving into the mysteries of health. This naturally evolved into a passion for health and wellness coaching, leading her to become a National Board Health and Wellness Coach (NBC-HWC). After enrolling in their program, she immersed herself in the coursework, refining her skills. She was invited to become part of their academy and now stand in front of eager minds, watching as students transformed before her eyes.
