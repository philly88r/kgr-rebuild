About our company

KGR is proud to be a female-owned business dedicated to empowering future health coach professionals. We are a NBHWC Approved Training Program who offers national board training for those looking to become NBHWC board certified health coaches. We also collaborate with community colleges to expand access to affordable health coaching education and support. In addition to our academy, KGR delivers a variety of coaching experiences tailored to both individuals and groups. Most importantly, when you join KGR, you become part of a family - because growth happens best when you're having fun!

Our Services

Training Program

Our NBHWC Approved Training Program is designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge needed to become a fully accredited NBC-HWC coach. Our motto is “Learning by Doing” which serves as the foundation of our program design. In addition, upon completion of our certifying course, enrollment includes full access to our comprehensive exam prep review course.

Individual & Group Coaching

KGR offers both individual and group coaching options for those who are ready to take charge of their wellbeing. For those seeking tailored guidance and focused support, one-on-one coaching offers the personalized approach you need. For those who thrive in a community setting, our Thrive Tribe communities offer a space dedicated to connection, growth, and encouragement.

Corporate Wellness

Interested in helping your employees get healthier and feel their best? KGR partners with businesses to enhance employee well-being through customized corporate health coaching. Our programs feature topics dedicated to stress management, sleep, nutrition, and exercise—providing practical tools that support lasting wellness.

"When employees are healthy, productivity rises and absenteeism drops, creating a win-win for everyone. Healthy employees are the foundation of strong, thriving workplaces!"
